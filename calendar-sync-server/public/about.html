Our project is focused on making a calendar that students can use to track assignments from various Learning Management Systems for all of their classes, ranging from Prairielearn to Moodle to Canvas. CalendarSync brings assignments from these platforms together, unifying your sources and producing a single, easy-to-use calendar for students. We hope you find it useful!
